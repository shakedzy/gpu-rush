GPU-TRON 3000

What’s on screen

You (pixel dev)
Move with arrows / WASD / drag. Collect GPUs, dodge everything else.
Green GPU
Collect to fill the quota bar (top-left). That’s how you clear a stage.
Gold GPU
Rare — instantly maxes your graphics quality.
Suits (agents)
Corporate buyers — touch them and they steal one of your GPUs.
Firewall Paywall TOS
Hazards drifting across the screen — all hurt on contact.
Medkit
Trade 2 GPUs for 1 extra life. Only appears from level 3 onward.
Boss
Big corp fight! It shoots legal projectiles. Fill your GPU quota, then each extra GPU damages the boss.
Breaking News
Pauses the game with a headline. Active effects show as icons with countdowns in the bottom-right.
Sell-out Offer
Press Y to sell out (2× score, game ends) or N to stay indie (no bonus, keep playing).
